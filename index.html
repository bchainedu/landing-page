Blockchain lectures, in Chinese

We found out that there is a large amount of blockchain related papers, articles, and videos in English. Few though, were available in Chinese and we aim to make them more easily accessible through our sites.

Free lectures

We strongly beleive that everyone has the right to learn about blockchain technology, for free. All lectures will be hosted on Youtube, VK, and Youku for everyone to benefit from.

Certification Exam in Chinese

Almost all of the existing blockchain certification exams are time-based online tests. We beleive this is where the language barrier hits the hardest and we want to change that. The certification exam will be offered as a paid serivce to those who seek careers related to blockchain.
